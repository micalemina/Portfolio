About the project

This project was a collaborative completed within a tight five-week timeframe. In my role as the UX/UI designer, I worked closely with the development team to bring our vision to life. Our goal was to create a furniture ecommerce app enhanced with augmented reality (AR) capabilities. Through close collaboration and teamwork, we successfully delivered a user-centric solution that redefined the online furniture shopping experience.
